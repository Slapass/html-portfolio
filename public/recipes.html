Green Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 30 oz chicken breast
  • 2 limes
  • 1x 16 oz green chili salsa
  • 1/2 cup orange marmalade
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions:

  • Slice chicken into strips, throw in pressure cooker
  • Almost cover chicken with water, add salt for tenderness
  • Pressure cook on high for 13 mins
  • Release pressure, drain water, shred chicken
  • Pour green salsa, marmalade, soy sauce, salt/garlic powder, 2 limes
  • Enjoy with tacos, nachos, enchiladas!
